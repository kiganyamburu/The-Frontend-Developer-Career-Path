Example Cafe Recipes World's Best Scrambles Eggs

I adapted this recipe from a book called Sydnay Food by Bill Grainger. Ever since tasting these eggs on my 1st visit to bill's restaurant in Kings Cross, Sydnet. I have been after the recipe. I have since transformed it int when i really believe are the best scrambled eggs i have ever tasted.
This recipe is wheat i call a "very special breakfast;" just look at the ingredients the see why. it has to be tasted to be believed.

Ingredients

The following ingridents make one serving

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1/ cup of cream (2 3/4 flounces)
  • a pinch of salt
  • Freshly milled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives(chopped)

    • Instructions

    1. 1. Whisk eggs, cream and salt in a bowl
    2. 2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a high heat(Taking care not to burn the butter).
    3. 3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait until it starts setting around the edge of the pan (around 20 seconds)
    4. 4. Using a wooden spartula, bring the mixure into the center as if ie were an omlet ands let it cook for another 20 seconds.
    5. 5. Fold contents in again, leave for 20 seconds , and repeat until the eggs are only just done
    6. 6. Grind a light sprinking of freshly milled pepper over the eggs and blend in some choppeed fresh chives

    You should only make maximum of two serving per frying pan
