Humans have reached Mars

The Starship rocket successfull landed on the red planet this morning

The Starship rocket, developed by SpaceX, has successfully landed on Mars this morning. The rocket was launched from the Kennedy Space Center in Florida, USA, on 1st July 2025. The rocket was carrying 100 passengers, including astronauts and scientists, who will be conducting various experiments on the red planet. The successful landing of the Starship rocket on Mars is a significant milestone in human space exploration and has opened up new possibilities for the future of space travel.
